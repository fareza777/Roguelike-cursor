# Privacy Policy — Veil of Abyss

**Effective date:** 2026-05-24  
**Developer:** fareza777 (solo indie)

## Summary

Veil of Abyss does **not** collect personal data on external servers. All saves are stored **locally** on your device.

## Data stored locally

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `user://meta.json` | Soul shards, upgrade levels |
| `user://codex.json` | Unlocked item lore entries |
| `user://run_save.json` | Optional suspended run (if enabled) |

## Third parties

- No analytics SDK in v1.0.0-rc1
- No ads in v1.0.0-rc1
- Google Play Games (optional, Fase 6 integration) — only if you enable it in a future update

## Children

Not directed at children under 13.
